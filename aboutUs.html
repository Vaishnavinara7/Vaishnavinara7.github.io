Company Overview


GeoPave Consultants Sdn Bhd is a specialist firm of consulting civil engineers, established in 2003 to provide geotechnical engineering and pavement engineering services to the civil engineering industry.


In response to the perceived need in the industry, the firm undertakes technical work solely in the field of geotechnical and pavement engineering. By practicing exclusively in this specialist niche within the civil engineering profession, the firm is able to deliver consulting services at the forefront of geotechnical and pavement technology to the highest standards.


GeoPave Consultants Sdn Bhd provides a full range of advice and services to a diverse client base including civil engineering consultants, contractors, research organization, government bodies, and various private organizations.
