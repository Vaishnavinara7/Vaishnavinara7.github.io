Consulting Services


Geotechnical


The range of services provided by GeoPave Consultants Sdn Bhd is both diverse and comprehensive. Provision of geotechnical support is catered for most civil engineering projects such as highways, railways, reclamation works, ports, power plants, bridges, high and low rise buildings, tunnelling etc.


Our areas of expertise and services provided in geotechnical works are as follows:


  • Geotechnical Investigation.
  • Geotechnical Interpretative Reports.
  • Foundations - shallow and deep foundation designs.
  • Ground Improvement Schemes.
  • Slope stability analysis (soil & rock) and slope stabilisation schemes.
  • Soil retention systems and tieback systems.
  • Tunnelling.
  • Ground water control and dewatering systems.
  • Earthworks quality control.
  • Geotechnical Instrumentation and interpretation.
  • Remedial works for foundation and slope failures.
  • Construction Supervision.
  • Preparation of drawings, specifications and reports.


Pavements


Specific consulting services are tailored to meet the need of the client. Our areas of expertise cover pavement design and rehabilitation works for roads, and heavy duty pavements for airfields, ports, industries and haul roads. Services provided are as follows:


  • Analysis and design of flexible and rigid pavements for roads, airfields, ports and industrial facilities.
  • Planning of geotechnical and pavement testing.
  • Pavement strength evaluation.
  • Development of rehabilitation strategies.
  • Design of rehabilitation works.
  • Design and specification for In-situ stabilization works.
  • Life cycle costing.
  • Preparation of drawings, specifications and reports.
  • Education and training.
